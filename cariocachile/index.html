What is Carioca?

Nine rounds, each with its own objective — from "two trios" all the way up to Escala Real without any leftover cards. Form trios and runs, race to lay your objective first, extend other players' melds, and chase the lowest total score across the full game. Quick games run 20–40 min and reward both opening luck and end-game patience.

What's inside

Multiplayer that's actually populated

Browse open public games at a glance, jump straight in with Quick Join, or host your own and pick when to start.

Six themes

Carioca, Dino, Tropical, Poker, Neon Arcade, Retro 8-bit. Pick a vibe, switch any time.

Game Center

Three leaderboards (wins, best score, win rate) and ten achievements to chase. Friends ranked side-by-side.

iCloud sync

Stats and saved games follow you across iPhone and iPad — no account required.
