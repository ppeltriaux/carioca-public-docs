Carioca Chile

The classic Chilean rummy, for iPhone and iPad.

Get it on the App Store Free with optional $2.99 in-app unlock — multiplayer is always free.

What is Carioca?

Nine rounds, each with its own objective: from "two trios" all the way up to "Escala Real" without any leftover cards. Form trios and runs, race to lay your objective first, extend other players' melds, and chase the lowest total score across the full game. Originally from Chile; now reborn for iOS.

Play any way you like

  • Solo vs CPU — three AI tiers (Easy, Medium, Hard) that actually think before discarding.
  • Multiplayer with friends — share a 6-character room code and play up to 4 humans in the same game from anywhere in the world. No accounts needed.
  • Mix & match — fill empty seats with CPUs when not everyone's online yet.

Six hand-crafted themes

Carioca (the signature green felt + gold accents), Dino, Tropical, Poker, Neon Arcade, and Retro 8-bit. Each ships its own card art, palette, fonts, and sound effects — pick the vibe you're in.

Built for iOS, with care

  • iCloud sync — settings, stats, and the game you're mid-way through travel automatically between your iPhone and iPad.
  • Four languages — English, Spanish, French, Portuguese.
  • Accessibility — Reduce Motion, dynamic text, sound + haptics toggles.
  • Portrait and landscape, with a polished iPad layout.

Privacy-first

No accounts. No tracking. No analytics. No ads. Local game state and stats stay on your device (and your iCloud, if you allow it). Multiplayer rooms self-delete 24 hours after the game ends. Read the privacy policy for the full details.

Transparent pricing

Play 3 full solo games for free to see if Carioca is your kind of game. After that, a one-time $2.99 unlocks unlimited solo play forever. Multiplayer stays free, always.
