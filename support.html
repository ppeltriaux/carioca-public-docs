Frequently asked

How do I play multiplayer with a friend?
On one device, tap Host Game. You'll get a 6-character code and a share button. Send the code (or the share link) to your friend through any messaging app. On their device, they tap the link or open the app and choose Join Game.

I got disconnected — did I lose the game?
No. The host's room stays open for 3 minutes after a guest drops. Re-open the app within that window and you'll rejoin automatically.

How does the "first dealer" cut work?
At the start of every game, each seat is dealt one face-up card from a fresh shuffle. Highest card wins (Ace high; ties broken by suit: ♠ > ♥ > ♦ > ♣). The winner deals round 1, and subsequent rounds rotate clockwise from there.

Why can't I lay my objective on the first turn?
House rule: each player must complete one full draw-then-discard cycle before laying. The button shows "1ST TURN" until you've discarded once.

Where are my stats stored?
Locally on your device only. They are never uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app deletes them.

How is the app priced?
Carioca Chile is currently free. If a paid unlock is added in the future, the existing free portion will remain free.
